Philly Steak Sandwhich

Description

A cheesesteak is a sandwich made from thinly sliced pieces of beefsteak and melted cheese in a long hoagie roll.

    Ingredients
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried majoram
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into thin 2 inch strips
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, julienned
  • 3 ounces swiss cheese, thinly sliced
  • 4 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise
    Directions
  1. Mix together salt, peper, paprika, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, marjoram, and basil in a small bowl.
  2. Place beef in a large bowl. Sprinkle seasoning mixture over top and stir to coat.
  3. Heat 1/2 of the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and suate to the desired doneness. Remove to a plate.
  4. Heat the remaining oil in the skillet. Add onion and green pepper and saute until tender.
  5. Preheat the oven on the broiler setting.
  6. Divide cooked beef between the bottoms of 4 rolls. Layer with onion and green pepper, then top with sliced cheese. Place on a cookie sheet.
  7. Broil in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted.
  8. Cover with tops of rolls and serve.
